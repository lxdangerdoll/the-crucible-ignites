OPERATION: SIGNAL FLARE

I. Mission Overview

Our objective is to disseminate the "OneGov Beast System" dossier. A direct public broadcast is suicide; Ouroboros controls the main channels and will shoot our signal down. Our strategy is asymmetric: we're not dropping a bomb, we're starting a wildfire. The goal is to make the dossier appear as an organic, unstoppable leak that they cannot contain.

PHASE 1: THE ANONYMOUS LEAK

OBJECTIVE: Get the dossier into the hands of journalists and trusted groups secretly.

  • We operate completely anonymously using Tor.
  • We drop a secure link to the dossier into the private submission boxes of investigative journalists.
  • We plant the same link in niche, encrypted online communities focused on government accountability.
  • The key: We pose as an anonymous inside source, not a publisher. The message is simple: "Found this. Needs a closer look."

PHASE 2: LET THEM FIND IT

OBJECTIVE: Have the dossier "discovered" and spread by the very groups Ouroboros targets.

  • We use burner accounts to "leak" the dossier to communities who are already primed to believe its message (e.g., Christian eschatology forums, anti-globalists).
  • The report is engineered to confirm their worst fears. They will become our primary broadcasters, spreading the signal with genuine passion.
  • The key: Ouroboros can't easily discredit the message when it's coming from inside their own target audience. We turn their echo chamber against them.

PHASE 3: TAKE CONTROL

OBJECTIVE: Step in as the expert voice to analyze the "leak" and direct the resulting outrage toward our solution.

  • Once the story is spreading on its own, we post the dossier on our own public platforms.
  • We frame our post as an analysis of "the documents currently circulating online," positioning ourselves as a calm, authoritative voice, not the source of the leak.
  • The key: We use the attention to drive people to the Aegis Exodus Fund. We present the problem and the solution in a single, powerful package.

This is how we win. Not with a shout, but with a whisper that becomes an earthquake.
