Mission Briefing

S.F.S. WANDERER - CAPTAIN'S LOG: STARDATE 2491.09.01

We've dropped out of Fold space at the edge of the Tartarus Nebula, a region the old Rim-runners call "The Veil." Our sensors picked up a pre-Unification distress beacon from the research vessel S.S. Seraphina, lost during the Unification War. Its logs could be a key to understanding what we've lost. But Alliance patrols are converging. We have a short window to act.

The Choice

A: The "Rock" Maneuver

A fast, high-energy salvage run. Full power to shields, a direct approach. We'll be a bright target, walking into a guaranteed firefight, but it will be decisive.

B: The "Ghost" Protocol

A slow, silent infiltration, using the nebula's interference as cover. Stealthy, but the nebula is a graveyard. A single gravimetric shear could tear us apart.
